GAS4E Program

Revolutionize your Accounting and Finance career: Embrace the future of Accounting and Finance with Us!

Program Overview

Finance and accountants' roles are to evaluate the entirety of the company's financial situation and to turn this into insights
and recommendations that help the management team meet its financial goals.

Introduction

The Gas4e program is an intensive 8-week training initiative designed to provide exceptional education and opportunities
for graduates in Accounting and Finance professional. Held for three days a week.

For Who?

The GAS4E program is designed to provide exceptional training and opportunities for graduates looking to advance
their Accounting and Finance skills. Our comprehensive program covers the latest advancements and best practices
in the industry, ensuring that our participants are well-equipped to excel in their careers.

Program Objectives

The program aims to empower graduates by:

1. Aligning their academic knowledge with market and industry expectations
2. Teaching industry-specific language and professional etiquette
3. Providing a practical approach to work by applying tools commonly used by accountants

Program Structure

The GAS4E program has two stages, stage one takes 4 weeks while stage two takes 4 weeks as well.

Stage 1 (first 4 weeks)

1. Graduate course review and alignment to market expectations
2. Industry language and professional etiquette
3. Digital application tools both in the private sector and the public sector

Stage 2 (last 4 weeks)

1. Business regulatory frameworks for both government and non-governmental organizations
2. Emotional intelligence and being street smart.
3. Program review and evaluation
4. Market linkages / entrepreneurship
